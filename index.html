Top 5 Favorite Artists

#1 Artist: Kodak Black

Kodak Black is the most fire artist by far and it would be amazing if he could stay out of jail and keep producing music.

#2 Artist: Pouya

Pouya is an amazing artist with lyrics that I would compare close to Kendrick Lamar. Album Five Five is arguably one of the greatest albums of all time.

#3 Artist: Flatbush Zombies

Flatbush Zombies was the first groups to really pull me to like rap music and the way they collaborate as a group is amazing.

#4 Artist: Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar was my favorite artist from day 1, although his newer music is less of my taste. Kendrick Lamar's older albums will always have a large place in my heart.

#5 Artist: Vince Staples

Vince Staples is another amazing lyricist that focuses on his words more than his beats giving him some amazing and very moving music.
